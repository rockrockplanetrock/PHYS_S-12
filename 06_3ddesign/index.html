3D Scanning

Part of our homework was to try out a 3D scanning app. I have an Android device, so I downloaded the top 3 apps in the Play Store. I'm not impressed with my results to say the least. I may try another free app, but the technology does not seem to be where I think it should be at this time. Look how wonky my attempts were below.
  • 3D Scanner for AR Core: not functional whatsoever.
  • display.land: could not render a soda can, but it did function. However, over the weekend the app informed me that it was no longer in use.
  • SCANN3D: definitely my best bet, but even after 5 attempts, I could not capture a small dog figurine.
